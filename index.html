About

A media buyer who reads P&Ls.

I started in social and content, but performance is where I found my edge. Over the last nine years I've operated inside the ad accounts that move money — building campaigns, killing what doesn't work, scaling what does, and reporting to executives in language they actually care about.

I've worked across fintech, real estate, e-commerce, sports, and medical, with campaigns running in Egypt, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and the wider GCC. I've built marketing departments from zero, led media buyer teams, and managed budgets where every dirham, riyal, and pound needed a job.

My approach is simple: test fast, kill faster, scale the winners. A/B frameworks, audience segmentation, full-funnel pacing, and creative briefing — it's the discipline that separates ad spending from ad investing.
